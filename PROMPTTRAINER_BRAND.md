# Javlin Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Essence
- **Name:** Javlin  
- **Tagline:** Practice. Feedback. Progress.  
- **Positioning:** AI-driven drill-based training for evaluator skills.

## 2. Voice & Tone
- **Personality:** Direct, supportive, expert.  
- **Tone:** Friendly, concise, active voice.  
- **Do:** “You’re on a roll—keep it up!”  
- **Don’t:** Use jargon or fluff.

## 3. Visual Identity

### Logo
- Wordmark “Javlin” in Montserrat Bold  
- Optional “J” icon with a diagonal cut for precision

### Color Palette
- **Charcoal:** `#1F2937`  
- **Azure Blue:** `#3B82F6`  
- **Emerald Green:** `#10B981`  
- **Scarlet Red:** `#EF4444`  
- **Light Gray:** `#F3F4F6`  
- **Dark Gray:** `#111827`

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat Bold  
- **Body:** Inter Regular

### Iconography
- 2px stroke line icons  
- Consistent corner radii (~4px)

## 4. Key Messaging Pillars
1. **Learn by Doing**  
2. **Instant Coaching**  
3. **Track & Celebrate**
